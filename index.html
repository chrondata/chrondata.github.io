Work With Us!

Our newsroom is awash in data — nonprofit financial statements, endowments, executive salaries, federal grants, IRS tax data, college graduation rates and more — and we need someone to help us organize and make sense of it all. We're seeking an entry-level database reporter to join our newsroom in Washington, D.C.

As part of the Data and Interactives team, the person in this position will focus on gathering data, especially on nonprofit groups, and increase The Chronicle’s ability to tell stories with data through multiple digital platforms, and will provide our audience with new and exciting ways to engage with Chronicle content.

This person will work with reporters across the newsroom, collaborating on individual stories, proposing new projects, and maintaining existing databases. The reporter will coordinate several of our high-profile annual projects, and will collaborate with designers and developers on data presentations.

The ideal candidate loves finding, gathering, and analyzing data to tell important stories, has an interest in the non-profit and higher education worlds, and is eager to use digital platforms to engage with readers.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities

  • Create and manage data-gathering surveys and data research projects.
  • Create and manage SQL databases, scrape data from websites and develop best practices for working with data so that reporters and editors are able to use data and statistics effectively and accurately in their stories.
  • Collaborate with reporters to find, analyze and understand data sets to be used as the basis for stories and in interactive news projects.
  • Provide guidance and tutelage for other reporters on using data and databases, and be a resource to others in The Chronicle newsroom who are interested in advancing their own technical skills related to computer-assisted reporting.
  • Work with the data and interactives team in an agile manner to produce innovative interactive projects, from the creation of wireframes and the generation of prototypes, through to final development and testing.
  • Participate in editorial brainstorming meetings to identify, discuss and plan projects.
  • Additional duties as assigned.

Requirements, Skills, Abilities

  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent of 3-5 years’ experience in database reporting.
  • Outstanding attention to detail is a must.
  • Experience performing critical data and statistical analyses and explaining findings clearly and succinctly.
  • Knowledge of a range of computer-assisted reporting tools, especially MySQL and Excel.
  • Ability to report and write clear and accurate stories, and conduct research on tax filings and through surveys.
  • Possess a strong work ethic, a commitment to meeting deadlines and a desire to expand knowledge.

How to Apply

Interested candidates should send a cover letter, résumé, and links to work examples to editorialposition@chronicle.com.
