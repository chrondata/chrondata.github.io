Data Editor

The Chronicle of Higher Education seeks a Data Editor to join its Data & Interactives team, who will work with both The Chronicle of Higher Education and The Chronicle of Philanthropy. The Data Editor is dedicated to finding, working with and analyzing data to  create both daily and enterprise stories and high-end, data-driven interactive projects for both publications. Working across the organization, this position will enhance The Chronicle’s ability to tell stories based on and with data through multiple digital platforms, create data assets of value to our readers, and will provide our audience with new and exciting ways to engage with Chronicle content.

Responsibilities will include, but are not limited to:

  • Create and manage SQL databases, scrape data from websites and other sources, and develop best practices for working with data so that reporters and editors are able to use statistics effectively and accurately in their stories.
  • Collaborate with reporters to find, analyze and understand data sets to be used as the basis for stories and in interactive news projects.
  • Provide guidance and tutelage for other reporters on using data and databases, and be a resource to others in The Chronicle newsroom interested in advancing their own technical skills related to computer-assisted reporting.
  • Work with the data and interactives team in an agile manner to produce innovative interactive projects.
  • Participate in editorial brainstorming meetings to identify, discuss and plan projects including, but not limited to, our annual executive compensation reports, Philanthropy surveys and various enterprise projects and stories.
  • Look for opportunities to optimize our work, especially as it applies to data collection and organization.
  • Additional duties as assigned.

The ideal candidate will have expertise in working with spreadsheets, databases (including MySQL), Python, Open Refine, mapping and other data analysis software. Candidates must be able to perform critical data and statistical analyses and explaining findings clearly and succinctly. Experience in database management and web scraping is required. Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent of 2-4 years’ experience in journalistic context.

Interested candidates should send a cover letter, resume, and salary requirements to datasearch@chronicle.com with “Data Editor” in the subject heading. No phone calls, please.
