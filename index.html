Work With Us!

We are hiring a News Apps Developer who will create large- and small-scale projects that help The Chronicle effectively present news and data online. This person will create engaging and informative data-driven news applications and multimedia narrative stories in collaboration with other members of the Data & Interactives team, reporters, and editors in both Chronicle newsrooms and with Chronicle's technology group.

Responsibilities will include, but are not limited to:

  • Work with Chronicle editors and reporters and with Chronicle technology staff to plan, design, develop, and promote interactive projects that will help us present complex information and data online.
  • Produce innovative and effective news applications and multimedia narratives in an iterative fashion.
  • Plan and execute online editorial projects, set milestones and deadlines, and collaborate with editors to set goals for these projects.
  • Work with the database reporters to analyze and visualize data for use in interactive news projects.
  • Consult with editors and other editorial staff to determine appropriate visualizations and other content for print editions.
  • Assist web producers in publishing and presenting editorial content on chronicle.com and philanthropy.com.

The ideal candidate will have excellent time and project management skills, with ability to multitask under a fast-paced environment.

Candidates must be comfortable working with and analyzing data.

Strong knowledge of and adherence to best practices in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and jQuery required. Familiarity with Django and AWS appreciated.

Bachelor's degree or equivalent of 1-3 years' experience in visual journalism, building interactive projects required.

Interested candidates should send a cover letter, resume, and salary requirements to editorialsearch@chronicle.com with "News Apps Developer" in the subject heading. No phone calls, please.
