We're the Data & Interactives team at The Chronicle of Higher Education and The Chronicle of Philanthropy. In a newsroom awash with data, we spend our days gathering, analyzing, visualizing and helping readers better understand the current climate of higher education and philanthropy. From financial statements, endowments, executive salaries, federal grants, and IRS tax data, to college graduation rates and more, there's always a story to be told.
