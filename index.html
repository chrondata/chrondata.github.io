Database Reporter

The Database Reporter is dedicated to finding, working with and analyzing the data that constitutes various stories and high-end, data-driven interactive projects for The Chronicle of Higher Education and The Chronicle of Philanthropy. As part of the Data and Interactives team, the person in this position will increase The Chronicle’s ability to tell stories with data through multiple digital platforms, and will provide our audience with new and exciting ways to engage with Chronicle content.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities

  • Create and manage SQL databases, scrape data from websites and develop best practices for working with data so that reporters and editors are able to use statistics effectively and accurately in their stories.
  • Collaborate with reporters to find, analyze and understand data sets to be used as the basis for stories and in interactive news projects.
  • Provide guidance and tutelage for other reporters on using data and databases, and be a resource to others in The Chronicle newsroom who are interested in advancing their own technical skills related to computer-assisted reporting.
  • Work with the data and interactives team in an agile manner to produce innovative interactive projects, from the creation of wireframes and the generation of prototypes, through to final development and testing.
  • Participate in editorial brainstorming meetings to identify, discuss and plan projects including, but not limited to, our annual executive compensation reports, Philanthropy surveys and various enterprise projects and stories.
  • Additional duties as assigned.

Minimum Job Requirements

Knowledge/Skills/Abilities
  • Demonstrated skillfulness in working with spreadsheets, databases (including MySQL), Python, Open Refine, mapping and other data analysis software.
  • Experience performing critical data and statistical analyses and explaining findings clearly and succinctly.
  • Experience with database management and web scraping.
  • Ability to report and write clear and accurate stories, and conduct research on tax filings and through surveys.
  • Possess a strong work ethic, a commitment to meeting deadlines and a desire to expand knowledge.
  • Excellent time management skills and ability to multitask.
  • Interest in or curiosity about higher education and the nonprofit world.
  • Work well in a collaborative team environment.
  • Experience with HTML, CSS, Javascript and jQuery is a plus.
Education
  • College degree or equivalent of 4-6 years’ experience in database reporting.
